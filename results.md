# Results from LCIA & MILP
### Mixed Integer Linear Programming (MILP) Model Results

* **Total GWP:** The optimized model achieved a total daily greenhouse gas emissions of **5,598 kg CO₂e**.
* **Fuel Consumption:** The model resulted in a daily fuel consumption of **271.7 liters**.
* **Time Metrics:**
    * **Total Travel Time:** 1,208 minutes.
    * **Total Idle Time:** 2,416 minutes.
* **Vehicle Assignments:**
    * **Total Assignments:** 1,208.
    * **Operating Vehicles:** 12 buses.
    * **Average Assignments per Vehicle:** 100.7.
* **Route Coverage:** The model successfully assigned the following number of vehicles to each route to meet the targets:
    * Commercial Area route: 4 vehicles.
    * Brunei route: 3 vehicles.
    * Boadi route: 2 vehicles.

***

### Life Cycle Impact Assessment (LCIA) Results

* **LCIA Baseline GWP:** The initial daily carbon footprint of the shuttle system was **5,877.3 kg CO₂e**.
* **Optimized GWP:** The MILP model reduced the GWP to **5,598 kg CO₂e**.
* **Reduction Potential:** This optimization represents a daily reduction of **279.3 kg CO₂e**, which is a **4.8% reduction** from the baseline.
* **Annual Reduction:** The potential annual reduction in carbon footprint is **101,950 kg CO₂e**.
* **Emission Hotspots:** The primary sources of emissions were identified as the Commercial Area Hub, Brunei Complex Bus Stop, mainly due to congestion and prolonged idling.
* **Carbon Intensity:** The optimized system's carbon intensity was calculated to be **0.00667 kg CO₂e per passenger-kilometer**.
